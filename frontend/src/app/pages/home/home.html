Government Benefits

Central & State Schemes for MSMEs

Unlock subsidies, grants and government support worth crores for your business

Central

PMEGP Scheme

Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme — up to 35% subsidy for new enterprises. Max project cost ₹50 Lakh for manufacturing.

Credit

CGTMSE Scheme

Collateral-free loans up to ₹2 Crore for MSMEs under Credit Guarantee Fund Trust. No third-party guarantee required.

Central

MUDRA Yojana

Loans up to ₹10 Lakh under Shishu, Kishore and Tarun categories for micro enterprises and small businesses.

Technology

Digital MSME Scheme

Cloud computing, ERP and digital solutions with government subsidy under the Digital MSME Scheme.

Marketing

Trade Fair Subsidy

Government subsidized participation in national and international trade fairs for MSME exporters and manufacturers.

Design

Design Clinic Scheme

50–75% subsidy for design consultancy services to improve product quality, packaging and marketability of MSME products.
