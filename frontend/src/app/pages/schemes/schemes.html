Government Schemes & Subsidies

Central and state government schemes worth crores for MSME growth

Central GovtUp to 35% subsidy

PMEGP — Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme

Financial assistance in the form of subsidy for setting up new micro enterprises in non-farm sector. Subsidy up to 25% in urban areas and 35% in rural areas.

  • Max project cost: ₹50 Lakh (manufacturing)
  • Max project cost: ₹20 Lakh (service)
  • Beneficiary contribution: 5-10% only
Credit GuaranteeCollateral Free

CGTMSE — Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for MSMEs

Provides collateral-free loans up to ₹2 Crore to micro and small enterprises. No third-party guarantee required from the borrower.

  • Loans up to ₹2 Crore without collateral
  • Covers manufacturing and service sectors
  • Both new and existing enterprises eligible
Central GovtUp to ₹10 Lakh

MUDRA Yojana — Micro Units Development & Refinance Agency

Provides loans up to ₹10 Lakh to non-corporate, non-farm small and micro enterprises under three categories: Shishu, Kishore, and Tarun.

  • Shishu: Up to ₹50,000
  • Kishore: ₹50,000 to ₹5 Lakh
  • Tarun: ₹5 Lakh to ₹10 Lakh
TechnologySubsidy Available

Digital MSME Scheme

Promotes adoption of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) tools by MSMEs. Provides subsidized cloud services and digital transformation support.

  • Subsidized cloud computing services
  • ERP and digital solution support
  • Training and capacity building
Marketing50% subsidy

Trade Fair Subsidy Scheme

Government assistance for MSME participation in national and international trade fairs, exhibitions and buyer-seller meets to promote their products globally.

  • 50% subsidy on stall charges
  • Domestic and international trade fairs
  • Exporter MSMEs especially eligible
DesignUp to 75% subsidy

Design Clinic Scheme

Brings Design expertise to Indian MSMEs for improving design capability and to provide access to expert design solutions to enhance product quality and market reach.

  • 50-75% subsidy on design consultancy
  • Product design and packaging
  • Manufacturing and service sectors
