Privacy Policy

How MSME Bharat Manch collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our platform and membership services.

MSME Bharat Manch (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy of MSME entrepreneurs, members, partners, and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you browse our website, register for membership, purchase plans, access business solutions, or download member reports—and how we handle that information responsibly and in line with applicable Indian law.

By creating an account, completing OTP verification, or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the platform.

1. Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

Account & identity information

  • Full name, email address, mobile number, and company/business name
  • Member ID (e.g. MBM format), account role, and membership status
  • Password (stored in encrypted/hashed form—we never store plain-text passwords)
  • Email and mobile OTP verification records and timestamps

Business & service information

  • Details you provide when applying for loans, subsidies, company formation, or other 360° solutions
  • Referral codes, plan selections, and communication preferences
  • Documents or data you upload where a service explicitly requires them

Payment information

  • Membership payments are processed through secure payment partners (e.g. Razorpay). We receive transaction IDs, amounts, and payment status—not full card or UPI credentials stored on our servers.

Reports & downloads

  • When credit or financial reports are generated for members, we store report metadata (upload date, file references, download counts) and associate them with your Member ID and account.
  • Report files are accessed only through authenticated login—not via public links.

Technical & usage data

  • IP address, browser type, device information, and log data for security and fraud prevention
  • Cookies and similar technologies for session management and site functionality (see Section 8)

2. How we use your information

We use personal information to:

  • Create and manage your member account and authenticate logins
  • Deliver membership benefits, invoices, and service recommendations
  • Process payments, subscriptions, renewals, and cancellations
  • Notify you when reports are ready and provide secure download access
  • Connect you with banks, NBFCs, government scheme facilitators, and growth partners where you request a service
  • Send service-related emails and SMS (OTP, payment confirmations, report notifications)
  • Improve platform security, troubleshoot errors, and comply with legal obligations
  • Send marketing communications only where permitted by law and your preferences

3. Member reports & credit-related data

MSME Bharat Manch may facilitate access to credit bureau reports, financial statements, or related documents for eligible members with an active subscription. Important points:

  • Reports are provided for your business use subject to bureau terms and applicable regulations.
  • Report ZIP/PDF files may be password-protected; password hints are communicated to your registered email.
  • We do not sell your credit report data to unrelated third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Authorized administrators upload reports to your account; download activity may be logged for audit and support.

You are responsible for safeguarding report passwords and downloaded files. Do not share reports publicly or with unauthorized parties.

4. When we share information

We may share information only as needed and with appropriate safeguards:

  • Service providers: Payment gateways, email/SMS delivery, hosting, and technology vendors bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Financial & scheme partners: When you explicitly request a loan, subsidy, or partner service, we share relevant business details to process your application
  • RupeeBoss ecosystem: As a national initiative associated with RupeeBoss.com, certain data may be processed within the RupeeBoss group for integrated MSME services, subject to this policy
  • Legal requirements: Courts, regulators, or law enforcement when required by applicable law

We do not rent or sell member contact lists to unrelated advertisers.

5. Security measures

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, hashed passwords, and restricted administrative access to member data and report files. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we encourage strong passwords and prompt reporting of suspected unauthorized access.

6. Data retention

We retain account and transaction data for as long as your membership is active and for a reasonable period thereafter for legal, tax, and dispute-resolution purposes. Report metadata and audit logs may be retained per internal policy. You may request account deactivation by contacting us; some records may be retained where required by law.

7. Cookies & similar technologies

We use essential cookies and local storage for login sessions and site preferences. Analytics or marketing cookies, if used, will be described in a cookie notice or consent banner where required. You can manage cookies through your browser settings; disabling essential cookies may affect login functionality.

8. Children’s privacy

Our services are intended for business owners and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age.

9. Your rights

Subject to applicable Indian law (including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, where applicable), you may:

  • Access and update profile information through your account or by contacting us
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent for optional marketing communications
  • Raise grievances regarding data processing with our support team

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect revisions. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
