Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my plan later? +
Yes, you can upgrade your membership plan at any time. You'll only pay the difference for the remaining period of your subscription. Contact our support team for seamless plan upgrades.
Is the Basic plan really free forever? +
Yes! The Basic plan is completely free with no time limit. You get full platform access, can apply for loans, discover government schemes, and attend events. Paid plans unlock priority support and dedicated relationship managers.
What is a Relationship Manager (RM)? +
A Relationship Manager is a dedicated MSME expert assigned to your account. They help you identify the right loan products, guide you through scheme applications, connect you with the right service providers, and provide personalized business advice.
How do I apply for government schemes through my membership? +
Once you're a member, you can browse all available government schemes, check your eligibility, and request application assistance. For Silver and above plans, our experts handle the complete application process on your behalf.
Is there a refund policy? +
We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with the service within the first 30 days, contact us and we'll process a full refund without any questions.
